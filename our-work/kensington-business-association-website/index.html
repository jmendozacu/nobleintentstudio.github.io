Kensington Talmadge Business Association

Easy-to-update website for the Kensington Business Association of Kensington, San Diego.

The Kensington Talmadge Business Association connects Kensington-based businesses with each other and other community organizations.

They needed help creating a new website to showcase all of their business members, highlight events they host, and offer ways for businesses and the community to connect with them and find more information about what they offer.

We designed and built a custom, Wordpress theme that allows them to customize and edit the information on the website as needed, post blog posts and events, and accept payments via their PayPal account.
