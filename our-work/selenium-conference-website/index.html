2015 Selenium Conference

Custom Wordpress website built in conjunction with Petre Spassov Design.

Design firm PS Design contacted us to help build the 2015 Selenium Conference Website. Using the Divi framework as a starting point, we built a completely customized Wordpress theme to power the site.

The site has custom sections showcasing the conference speakers, schedule of events and other conference information.

We worked closely with PS Design throughout the development process to ensure the finished site matched the design they created and the branding they designed for the 2015 conference (see it here).
