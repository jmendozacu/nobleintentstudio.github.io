Cornerstone Cleaning & Restoration

Website and printed marketing materials for Chicago-based cleaning services company Cornerstone Cleaning & Restoration.

Cornerstone, a cleaning and restoration company based in the Chicago-suburbs, came to us with an existing logo, but needed help with their marketing materials and website.

To get started, we designed a business identity system including business cards, letterhead, and envelopes.

We then designed and wrote marketing copy for a set of four brochures highlighting Cornerstone's unique services.

We began to design Cornerstone's website at the close of 2011. We wrote fresh copy for the new website and also designed a mobile optimized version of the site, knowing many of their potential customers would be accessing the site on-the-go.

Go to the site.

Since then, we’ve designed stickers, magnets, coupons, and more for Cornerstone’s marketing outreach.
